Discover Atlantica

Atlantica City is a seaside resort city in New Jersey. It's located on the Atlantica Ocean in Atlantic County. Atlantic City is known for its boardwalk, beachfront, and gaming scene. It was a popular destination for the rich and famous between the 1930s and 1950s.
